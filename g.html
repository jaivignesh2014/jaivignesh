Stay Safe from Viruses, Malware and Online Scams.

How to Prevent Viruses on Android

  • Install apps only from Google Play Store.
  • Keep Android updated.
  • Enable Google Play Protect.
  • Use strong passwords.
  • Do not click unknown links.
  • Do not download unknown APK files.
  • Avoid connecting to unsafe Wi-Fi networks.
  • Check app permissions before installing.
  • Delete unused apps regularly.
  • Back up important data.
